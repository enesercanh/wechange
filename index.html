About We Change

We Change is a nonprofit organization committed to empowering Iraqi youth through extracurricular activities, research programs, and career development opportunities. Our mission is to inspire and support the next generation of leaders.

Our Activities

  • Charity events to support local communities.
  • Research programs to foster innovation.
  • Educational trips for experiential learning.
  • Book clubs to promote reading culture.
  • Career experience opportunities for students.
